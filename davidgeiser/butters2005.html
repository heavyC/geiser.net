Red Flower Fire I, 2005
Mixed media on panel.
40" x 40"
$4,600.00 		One of the original "Butters Gallery artists", David Geiser has been captivating Oregonians with his paintings for over 17 years. Geiser's rough but refined signature style is unique and relentless. Not for the meek, these works are passionate and exuberant expressions of body, mind, and spirit while remaining obliquely abstract. Working within his repertoire of textures and colors Geiser creates works that inspire one to dive in and explore the slippery pools of varnish juxtaposed with the dense powdery fields and burnished passages of gold or copper.

To behold a David Geiser painting is to connect most directly with what paint can do to reflect the essence of life.
