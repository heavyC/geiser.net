David Geiser continues to delight viewers at Butters Gallery with his signature organic, textural paintings where process and rendering collide and unite. The newest paintings display a slight stylistic shift--possessing a more distinct clarity of space. Geiser continues to explore and expand his color arrangements, but always within the realm of natural occurrence--even those we may not encounter every day.

From New York, gallery mainstay David Geiser makes mixed media paintings that are very physically present. Highly emotive, these works are replete with complex color arrangements that are compelling--whether a small "chunk" or a massive composition.
