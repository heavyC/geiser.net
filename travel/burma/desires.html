The People's Desires

I saw this an English-language billboard in central Rangoon. You may never see the word "Stooges" used without irony anywhere else. I swear this is a verbatim copy of the text.

Interestingly, there was no Burmese translation in sight

People's Desire

* Oppose those relying on external elements, acting as stooges, holding negative views

* Oppose those trying to jeopardize stability of the State and progress of the nation

* Oppose foreign nations interfering in internal affairs of the state

* Crush all internal and external destructive elements as the common enemy
